Currency and Commodity Course

Duration: 10 hours +

Course Fees: Rs 10,000 per person

Master Forex and Commodity Trading

Learn the fundamentals of forex and commodity trading, including technical analysis, risk management, and trading strategies. This course is designed for traders who want to explore global markets and diversify their portfolios.

Course Contents:

  • Introduction to Forex Trading: Understand how the forex market works and its key components.
  • Forex Market Basics: Learn about pips, points, lots, leverage, and short selling.
  • Forex Terminology: Familiarize yourself with essential forex terms.
  • Technical Analysis: Use candlestick patterns, chart patterns, volume, and technical indicators for trading.
  • Fundamental Analysis: Analyze economic indicators and news events that impact currency prices.
  • Order Types: Understand different order types and how to use them effectively.
  • Risk Management: Learn how to manage risk and protect your capital.
  • Portfolio Management: Diversify your portfolio with forex and commodity trading.
  • Money Management: Manage risk on each position, determine position sizing, and set stop-loss levels.
  • Commodity Trading: Explore trading in commodities like gold, silver, oil, and agricultural products.
  • Case Studies: Apply trading strategies to real-world examples.

By the end of this course, you will have the skills to trade forex and commodities confidently. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced trader, this course will help you navigate global markets and achieve your financial goals.
