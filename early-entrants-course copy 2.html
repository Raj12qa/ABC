1. Financial Concepts

Learn about equity, debentures, mutual funds, and their types. Understand the basics of financial instruments and their role in the market.

2. Introduction to Stock Markets

Get an overview of how stock markets operate, their purpose, and their significance in the global economy.

3. Concept of Large Caps, Mid Caps, Small Caps

Understand the classification of companies based on market capitalization and their investment potential.

4. Understanding the Components of the Share Market

Explore the key components of the share market, including exchanges, indices, and market participants.

5. Fundamental Analysis and Ratio Analysis

Learn how to evaluate companies using fundamental analysis and key financial ratios.

6. Difference Between Fundamental & Technical Analysis

Understand the key differences between fundamental and technical analysis and when to use each approach.

7. Introduction to Technical Analysis

Discover the basics of technical analysis, including chart patterns, trends, and indicators.

8. Dow Theory

Learn the principles of Dow Theory and how it forms the foundation of technical analysis.

9. Different Types of Orders

Understand the various types of trading orders, such as market orders, limit orders, and stop-loss orders.

10. Understanding Charts & Types of Charts

Explore different types of charts, including line charts, bar charts, and candlestick charts.

11. Introduction to Candlestick Patterns

Learn how to read and interpret candlestick patterns to predict market movements.

12. Introduction to Trading Platforms/Software/Brokers

Get familiar with popular trading platforms, software, and brokers to start your trading journey.
