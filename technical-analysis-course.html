Technical Analysis Course

Duration: 10 hours +

Course Fees: Rs 10,000 per person

Master the Art of Technical Analysis

Learn how to analyze price charts, identify trends, and make informed trading decisions with our Technical Analysis Course. This course is designed for traders who want to gain a deep understanding of technical analysis tools and techniques.

Course Contents:

  • Financial Concepts: Understand equity, debentures, mutual funds, and their types.
  • Introduction to Stock Markets: Learn about large caps, mid caps, and small caps.
  • Components of the Share Market: Understand the key elements that drive stock prices.
  • Fundamental Analysis vs. Technical Analysis: Learn the differences and when to use each approach.
  • Ratio Analysis: Analyze financial ratios to evaluate a company's performance.
  • Peer Group Analysis: Compare companies within the same industry.
  • Stock Screening: Identify potential investment opportunities using screening tools.
  • Introduction to Technical Analysis: Learn the basics of technical analysis and its importance.
  • Dow Theory: Understand the principles of Dow Theory for market analysis.
  • Types of Orders: Learn about market orders, limit orders, stop-loss orders, and more.
  • Understanding Charts: Explore line charts, bar charts, and candlestick charts.
  • Candlestick Patterns: Master single, two, and three-candlestick patterns for reversal and continuation.
  • Momentum Indicators & Oscillators: Use RSI, MACD, and other indicators for trading signals.
  • Volume Analysis: Understand the role of volume in confirming trends.
  • Bollinger Bands: Learn to use Bollinger Bands for volatility analysis.
  • Trading Platforms: Get introduced to different trading platforms, software, and brokers.

By the end of this course, you will have the skills to analyze charts, identify trends, and execute trades with confidence. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced trader, this course will help you elevate your trading game.
